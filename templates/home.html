About me

Currently a Product Designer for enterprise and consumer web applications at Hightail, I am also a graduate of the University of Michigan School of Information. I have a Master of Science in Information with specializations in Human-Computer Interaction and Information Economics.

I have an enduring fascination with people and their intersection with technology. My current interests include applications related to pervasive (aka ubiquitous) computing and computer-supported cooperative work.

What I enjoy most is digging deeply into the lives of people and applying my creativity to create elegant solutions.
